# Privacy Policy — Anatomy Atlas

**Last updated:** 2 May 2026

## Overview
Anatomy Atlas ("the App") is an offline anatomy education reference. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

## Data we do NOT collect
- We do not collect any personal information.
- We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking.
- We do not require a user account or login.
- We do not connect to external servers during normal use.
- We do not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other device capability.

## Local storage
The App may store your last-viewed page in browser / app local storage to restore your session. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-party services
None. All content is bundled within the App.

## Children
The App does not knowingly collect data from children or any user.

## Changes
If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will be revised. Because no data is collected, changes are unlikely.
